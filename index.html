🧠 Coach Review

❓ How Chess Coach Works

Three Different Help Systems

Coach Mode gives educational commentary, warnings, and post-game review.

Visual Hints are the green move dots and red capture rings that appear when you select a piece.

Need a Hint gives a text suggestion about ideas to consider without playing the move for you.

Interaction Modes

Passive speaks only on major tactical or strategic moments.

Guided gives more frequent teaching during the game.

Review is the deepest teaching mode and is best paired with post-game analysis.

Settings

Show Coach Says Messages turns the coach text panel on or off.

Coach Voice uses your browser's built-in speech synthesis for spoken advice.

Motivational Messages lets the coach encourage you when there is no urgent warning.

Threat Reminders makes the coach call out loose pieces, danger, and king-safety issues.

Persistence

Your progress rating, unfinished game, and saved reviews are stored locally in your browser so you can return later.

🧩 Puzzle Guide

Puzzle Modes

Practice Puzzle lets you train by tier, category, pack, favorites, theme, or rating range from Puzzle Hub.

Daily Puzzle gives you one locally selected puzzle per day based on your current puzzle rating.

Puzzle Rush is a timed run that keeps serving new puzzles until time expires or the run ends.

Puzzle Duel is a local two-player race where both players compete through the same sequence of puzzles.

Browsing And Favorites

Puzzle Hub includes curated packs, your saved favorites, and the full All Puzzles browser.

Favorites let you bookmark puzzles for later and replay them from the dedicated favorites section.

Browser filters let you search by tier, category, theme, pack, rating range, and favorites-only combinations.

Puzzle Controls

Retry Puzzle resets the current position so you can solve it cleanly again.

Puzzle Hint gives a guided nudge without auto-playing the move for you.

Show Solution reveals the full line when you want to study the answer directly.

Next Puzzle loads another puzzle from the current mode or training context.

How Puzzle Help Works

Coach Mode and Visual Hints remain separate toggles during puzzle play.

Mistakes counts incorrect tries on the active puzzle.

Progress such as rating, favorites, completions, and achievements is stored locally so you can come back later.
